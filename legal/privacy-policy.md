### 1. Information Collection

We use third-party analytics services (Umami Analytics) to collect anonymous visitor data, including but not limited to: page views, referral sources, browser type, operating system, and geographic location (country/region level).

We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII). All collected data is anonymized to ensure it cannot be traced back to specific individuals.

### 2. Data Usage

Collected data is used solely for the following purposes:

- Improving website content and user experience
- Analyzing traffic and usage patterns
- Ensuring site security and performance

We pledge not to use data for commercial marketing purposes, nor will we sell, rent, or otherwise transfer it to any third party. Data usage is strictly limited to the aforementioned purposes, with appropriate technical and organizational measures implemented to protect data security.

### 3. Cookie Usage

We use essential cookies to save your preference settings, including theme mode, language selection, and privacy consent status. These cookies are critical to website functionality.

Umami Analytics employs cookie-less tracking technology and does not store any tracking identifiers on your device. You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect certain website functions.

### 4. Data Storage & Cross-Border Transfer

Analytics data is stored on Umami Cloud servers, which comply with EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) standards. Due to the global nature of the internet, servers may be located in different countries or regions, and your data may be transferred and stored across borders.

We ensure all data transfers use industry-standard encryption technology (TLS/SSL) and enter into data protection agreements with processors requiring compliance with applicable data protection laws.

### 5. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

- Withdraw consent to data processing at any time
- Request access to data we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- Object to non-essential data collection
- Data portability

Due to our anonymized data collection approach, we cannot identify specific users' data and may be unable to fully satisfy certain personal data requests. To exercise these rights, please contact us through the methods provided on the website.

### 6. Minor Protection

This website is not specifically directed at children under 13 (or other ages as defined by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

If we discover that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a minor, we will immediately take steps to delete the relevant data. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us promptly.

### 7. Legal Compliance

This Privacy Policy is formulated and executed in compliance with:

- EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
- Personal Information Protection Law of the People's Republic of China
- Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region
- Other applicable data protection laws

We regularly review and update our privacy policy to ensure continued compliance.

### 8. Policy Changes

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any significant changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date at the top.

For substantial changes, we will notify you through website announcements or other appropriate means. We recommend reviewing this policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your information. Continued use of the website constitutes acceptance of the revised privacy policy.

### 9. Contact Us

If you have any questions, comments, or complaints about this Privacy Policy, or need to exercise your data rights, please contact us through the methods provided on the website. We will respond to your request as soon as possible (typically within 30 days) upon receipt.
